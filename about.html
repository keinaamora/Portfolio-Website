About

My Story

At age 12, I moved from Cebu City, Philippines to Cherry Hill, New Jersey. Growing up, I have always been interested in colorful objects and photography. Technology did not play a huge part of my childhood since I grew up in a third-world country and my parents were poor. But now living in the United States, I want to improve people’s lives by presenting my passion of design using technology.

In my free time, I enjoy baking desserts, finding inspirations online, spending time with my friends, and working out. I also love travelling and have a 30+ souvenir hats from my travels and my family’s travels.

After I graduated high school, I went to community college, intending to transfer to a four-year university to study Earth Sciences. However, the very interesting classes I took in college pulled me in trying different majors. After discovering what UX Design is, I immediately signed up for CareerFoundry’s UX Design course. My main inspiration for wanting to be a UX Designer are the many confusing apps, websites, and POS Systems that I have encountered and the people who use them. Somehow, someway, I want users to easily reach their goals and needs without any confusion. In April 2020, I completed CareerFoundry’s UX Immersion course. Currently, I am taking their Frontend Development for Designers course that will teach me the basics of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
